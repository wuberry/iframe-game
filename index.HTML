About Snake vs Block

Snake vs Block is a modern twist on the classic snake game that you loved as a kid. This game combines the nostalgia of the original snake concept with exciting new challenges and features.

The objective is simple: control your snake to eat as much food as possible without hitting the walls or the blocks that appear on the screen. As you eat more food, your snake grows longer, making the game progressively more challenging.

With multiple levels, power-ups, and a competitive score system, Snake vs Block offers endless hours of entertainment. Compete with friends to see who can achieve the highest score and become the ultimate snake master!

  • Smooth controls and responsive gameplay
